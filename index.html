Five Pillars of Research

In Vitro

Monolayer cultures (fibroblasts, ECs, pericytes, iPSC-derived macrophages, PBMCs), organotypic models (3D skin equivalents, cardiac microtissues, adipoids), organoids (skin, lung, blood vessel), and organ-on-chip (skin, lung, combinations).

🔬

Ex Vivo

Precision-cut tissue slices from patient biopsies preserving native tissue architecture, cellular diversity, and microenvironment for direct drug testing.

In Vivo

Bleomycin-induced dermal and pulmonary fibrosis, cGvHD, Topoisomerase-I-induced SSc, HisRS-induced ASyS, CCl4-induced liver fibrosis, UUO kidney fibrosis, and more.

View details
🧪

OMICs-Based Profiling & Bioinformatics

Xenium spatial transcriptomics, single cell/nucleus RNA-seq, Imaging Mass Cytometry, ChIP-seq, ATAC-seq, Perturb-seq, Ribo-seq, Bulk RNA-seq, and custom bioinformatic analyses.

🏥

Clinical Trial Sample Profiling

Histology, IF/IHC staining of targets, OMICs-based profiling, and FACS analysis of samples from clinical trials — bridging preclinical insights with clinical outcomes.

ABOUT US

Dedicated to Scientific Excellence

4D Science GmbH is a leading non-clinical Contract Research Organization specializing in systemic sclerosis, fibrosis, and myositis research. We collaborate closely with pharmaceutical and biotechnology companies to develop and validate new therapeutic approaches.

WHAT WE DO
Comprehensive Research Services

Our team provides comprehensive research services including in vitro, ex vivo, and in vivo models, biomarker discovery, and mechanistic studies tailored to systemic sclerosis and related fibrotic diseases.

We coordinate cutting-edge OMICs technologies and methodologies to advance the understanding and treatment of fibrosis and autoimmune diseases, aiming to improve patient outcomes and quality of life.

Our state-of-the-art model portfolio and rigorous quality standards make us a trusted partner in the journey towards effective treatments for these complex diseases.
